About BucksCoJS

Bucks County JS is a group of professionals that are interested in, and involved with development in the browser and beyond. We usually meet on the first Wednesday of every month. Topics include JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, build tools and automation, popular/upcoming frameworks and libraries, and more!

All participants are expected to follow the JSConf Code of Conduct.
